About the career I chose

The career I chose for today is engineering. There are multiple types of engineering, the specific type of engineering I chose today is software engineering. The responsibilities of this job are quite straight forward, it revolves around creating computer systems software and applications. The education required for this career path is a bachelor's degree in computer systems engineering, software engineering or mathematics or completion of a college program in computer science. A master's or doctoral degree may also be required. With the growing innovation of hardware and software, the demand for software engineers couldn't be higher right now, this career path can lead you to many places. The average base salary of a software engineer is $76 448 in Canada. The reason why I'm interested in this career is because I have always had an interest in computers and the software in which they run on. Choosing a path in software engineering ensures that I get to learn more about something that I am passionate in.


The university I chose is Waterloo. The entrance requirements are that you have done the following Grade 12 U/M courses with at least a 70% grade:

  • Advanced Functions
  • Calculus and Vectors
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • English (ENG4U)

Other courses that may help are:

  • Grade 11 U Introduction to Computer Science
  • Grade 12 U Computer Science

The first year courses you will take are:

  • Arts
  • Engineering
  • Environment
  • Health
  • Mathematics
  • Social Work
  • Science
  • Interdisciplinary Studies

Usually, a software engineer will be receiving education for three to four years. This career could help everybody because software engineers can develop software that can help people with disabilities or help students learn, etc. The possibilities are endless.
